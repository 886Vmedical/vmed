Our Pledge to You

We pledge to respect your privacy, to be transparent about our data practices, to keep your data safe, to never sell your personal data, to let you decide how your information is shared, and to only collect the data that helps us improve our products and services for your benefit.

What We Collect

We only collect personal information, such as gender, date of birth, height and weight to make sure the readings you receive from us are accurate and complete.

Why We Collect Data

We’re not in the data business, we’re in the health and fitness business. Any data we collect from you is specifically designed to ensure that you receive detailed and accurate results from each of your tests. Its important to collect information to help us improve our products and services. This information helps us create a better user experience. And as our customer, you deserve our best.

We Do Not Sell Personal Data

We don’t sell data that could identify you to anyone, anywhere, or anytime. We only share it if you tell us to or if we’re required to so by law.
